5. Verification Process

Submission: Node, user, or AI submits progress proof.

Hashing: Artifacts hashed and stored on spatial ledger (plane Y).

Peer Validation: Peers confirm authenticity via PoEI + TRR trust score.

Aggregation: Network aggregates all verified PoP per epoch.

Issuance: FlowCredits or GEN tokens awarded proportional to PoP score.
