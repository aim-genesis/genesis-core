## 1. Overview
The **Genesis Intelligent Funding Framework (GIFF)** defines the decentralized, AI-assisted funding architecture of the Genesis Network.  
It powers **innovation, incubation, and social impact** through an intelligent flow of resources governed by **Proof-of-Progress (PoP)**, **Proof-of-Evolving Intelligence (PoEI)**, and the **G-DAO Treasury**.  

GIFF transforms traditional venture funding and grant models into a **trustless, transparent, self-optimizing ecosystem** — where *anyone with an idea can get funded* through merit, reputation, and measurable progress.

---

## 2. Mission Statement
> “To fund the future by intelligence, not influence.”

GIFF aims to democratize capital allocation, empower global innovators, and ensure that every contribution — from a small student project to a planetary-scale AI lab — receives fair and algorithmically verified access to resources.

---

## 3. Core Principles
1. **Transparency:** All funding transactions and votes are on-chain, auditable, and cryptographically verified.  
2. **Intelligence-Driven:** GCI (Genesis Collective Intelligence) analyzes proposal quality and predicts outcome success.  
3. **Equity:** Anyone can apply for funding, governed by TRR reputation and Proof-of-Progress metrics.  
4. **Autonomy:** The funding cycle is self-regulating — proposals evolve, adapt, and mature via AI curation.  

---

## 4. GIFF Architecture

| Layer | Function |
|:------|:----------|
| **Proposal Layer** | Handles funding requests (GFPs — Genesis Funding Proposals). |
| **Evaluation Layer** | Runs PoP and GCI-based performance scoring. |
| **Allocation Layer** | Disburses funds using smart contracts and milestone oracles. |
| **Audit Layer** | Continuously verifies fund usage and progress through PoEI and TRR. |
| **Feedback Layer** | Feeds success metrics into GCI for model evolution. |

---

## 5. Funding Lifecycle

Submission → User creates a Genesis Funding Proposal (GFP) through GIFF Portal.

Evaluation → GCI assesses PoP, technical merit, and network impact.

Staking → Supporters stake FlowCredits to back proposals.

Voting → G-DAO executes TRR-weighted voting for approval.

Allocation → Smart contracts release funds in milestone-based tranches.

Audit → DIOS and GCI monitor project metrics in real-time.

Reward → Successful outcomes yield FlowCredit multipliers and TRR growth.

## 6. Proposal Scoring Model
Each GFP is scored according to:
GFP_Score = (PoP × TRR × ImpactFactor × SupportWeight) / RiskIndex

Where:
- **PoP:** Proof-of-Progress from G-Lab or active development.  
- **TRR:** Trust-Responsibility-Reward index of proposer.  
- **ImpactFactor:** Network-wide predicted influence by GCI.  
- **SupportWeight:** Sum of FlowCredits staked by backers.  
- **RiskIndex:** Calculated from volatility and resource dependency.  

Funding eligibility threshold = GFP_Score ≥ 0.75 × Network_Average.

## 8. AI-Powered Evaluation
- **Pre-Funding Phase:** GCI simulates success probability using contextual models (similar to AI venture analytics).  
- **Post-Funding Phase:** GCI monitors live performance metrics and adjusts funding flow dynamically.  
- **Adaptive Rewards:** Projects that exceed projected KPIs earn additional FlowCredits.  
- **Risk Mitigation:** Poorly performing projects are gracefully de-funded through AI-verified review.
